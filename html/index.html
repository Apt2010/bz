Frequently Asked Questions about our BZTurk Extension

BZTurk is an innovative browser extension designed to boost the earnings and productivity of Amazon Mechanical Turk (MTurk) workers. It automates the task acceptance process using advanced machine learning algorithms, allowing users to efficiently find and accept Human Intelligence Tasks (HITs).

  • Install the BZTurk Extension: Download and install the extension from the Chrome Web Store.
  • Obtain a BZTurk License : Sign up for a license and link it to your MTurk Worker ID.
  • Set Your Preferences : Customize your filter settings to add your favorite HITs and block unwanted ones.
  • Save Your Settings : Double-check and save your custom filter preferences.
  • Start the Finder : Activate the finder to automatically accept HITs that match your criteria.
  • Manual HIT Catcher : For additional control, you can manually add HITs to your list.
  • Enable the "Auto HIT Catcher" toggle in your filter settings to automatically capture HITs based on your minimum reward criteria.
